Weddings — 2026

In 2026 Budots Media filmed the wedding of Cinduy Cinco — a production that carried everything the studio does into a single day: cinema cameras on the ground, drones in the air, and an edit that treats a wedding like an expedition film.

Wedding work is where the production house's disciplines converge. The same drone pilots who tracked outrigger canoes around Cebu fly the processional; the same editors who cut DOT campaigns grade the golden hour; same-day edits are assembled on site so the film premieres at the reception.

A wedding is a one-take documentary. There is no second sunrise, no reshoot, no B-roll day. It is the most honest test of a film crew that exists.

For Cinduy's films, the brief was Cebuano at heart and cinematic in scale — story first, family everywhere in the frame, the island as the third character.
