Growth Market

Since the post-ChatGPT pivot, Budots Media has named three growth markets: Arabia, India and Southeast Asia. This page is the India door — what a Cebu production house with a decade of resort, event and aerial work offers Indian clients, five flight-hours away.

Destination Weddings

India built the world's greatest destination-wedding culture; Cebu built the resorts for it. We film weddings the way we film expeditions — cinema cameras on the ground, drones over the ceremony, same-day edits premiering at the reception — at the properties we already know shot by shot: NUSTAR, JPARK, the Mactan resort strip. See the Cindy Cinco wedding at NUSTAR and our Brides at Triton expo — the wedding industry event we have produced since 2012.

The GCC Coast

India invented the Global Capability Center; Cebu is where the model lands next. We covered the launch of the Philippines' first AI-led GCC — Ascendion × PhilTech in Cebu — and our client wall already reads like a GCC directory: Accenture, JPMorganChase, Conduent, Foundever, Ascendion. Aerial & Survey

Our Aerial Data Ops portfolio spans Riyadh's Diriyah site, Dubai, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur and the Philippine islands — licensed drone operations, photogrammetry, laser scans and gaussian splats, delivered as survey products and cinematic footage. The same capability travels.

Sovereign AI, Parallel Roads

India is building sovereign AI for its languages through IndiaAI and Bhashini; Budots Media is building a sovereign Filipino language model for the 180+ languages of the Philippines. Two countries, the same conviction: a nation's digital life should run on models that understand its languages and answer to its institutions. We see Indian AI teams as natural collaborators — on data, evaluation and multilingual production pipelines.

Five flight-hours apart, the same ocean, the same instinct: build it at home, film it beautifully, show the world.
